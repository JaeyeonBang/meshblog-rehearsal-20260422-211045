title: Daily Repair
date: 2026-04-21
tags: [workflow]
---

Daily repair is the habit of running a short audit over your notes before
closing the laptop. Typical steps:

1. Flip `draft: true` notes to public when ready.
2. Resolve broken wikilinks.
3. Merge orphaned one-liners into stronger notes.
